What is Wildlife photography?

Wildlife photography is a type of photography that focuses on capturing different types of wildlife in their natural surroundings. Wildlife photographers may need field craft skills in addition to photography talents. Some animals and birds, for example, are difficult to approach, necessitating knowledge of the animal's or bird's behavior in order to forecast their activities. Stalking skills or the use of a hide/blind for concealment may be required while photographing some species.
